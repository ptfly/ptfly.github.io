Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

MCBG ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application for the motorcycle club community in Bulgaria. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use MCBG, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number, and club membership, provided during registration.
  • User-generated content — messages, events, and other content you create within the app.
  • Device information — push notification token, device type, and operating system version, used to deliver notifications.
  • Location data — your approximate location, only when you explicitly grant permission, used to display nearby clubs on the map.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality.
  • To send push notifications about new events, messages, and club updates.
  • To display club locations on the interactive map.
  • To manage your club membership and permissions.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers provided by SashiDo (a Parse Server hosting platform). Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — for delivering push notifications. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Maps SDK — for displaying maps on Android. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Apple MapKit — for displaying maps on iOS. Subject to Apple's privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with other app users as part of the app's intended functionality (e.g., your name and club are visible to other members).

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

MCBG is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via your device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
